About

A dojo is the portal on Web3 for the metaverse. Users can participate in viewing NFTs, gamified marketing campaigns, challenges, promotions or mining activities.

Problems we aim to solve

  • NFT owners and Metaverse early adopters need a blockchain-backed 3D space to display and market their collections and socialize with like-minded people.
  • Mainstream businesses and organizations need new ways of engaging the digital native generation by gaining Web 3.0 presence and build branding in the Metaverse in a cost-effective and sustainable manner.

How do we do it

  • 3D rich visuals + user avatar & skin
  • NFT display & minting
  • Marketplace
  • Branding customizations, virtual meeting rooms, assistance bots, and many more

A Dojo

Organisations or individuals who are interested in web3 real estates and business models can buy a prebuilt dojo on the land or build their own on their land. Users can participate in different activities offered by a dojo. Dojos are also the entrance to the services of the organizations. e.g. Gallery

DAO

You can enable decetralized organizations for your dojo allowing a community-driven future.

Opportunities

Token drop, NFT drop, learn to earn and promotions.

Web3 Campaigns

A business can develop their meta dojo to run campaigns such as play to earn and learn to earn. leads and users are incentivized to participate.
