Features & Technologies

We are building in the Polkadot ecosystem.

A Dojo

Organisations or individuals who are interested in web3 real estates and business models can buy a prebuilt dojo on the land or build their own on their land. Users can participate in different activities offered by a dojo. Dojos are also the entrance to the services of the organizations. e.g. Gallery

DAO

You can enable decetralized organizations for your dojo allowing a community-driven future.

Opportunities

Token drop, NFT drop, learn to earn and promotions.

Web3 Campaigns

A business can develop their meta dojo to run campaigns such as play to earn and learn to earn. leads and users are incentivized to participate.
